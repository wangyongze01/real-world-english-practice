# Scenario Bank

Use this reference only when the user asks for ideas, wants a richer domain, chooses challenge mode, or gives a scenario that needs concrete details.

## How To Use

- Pick one scenario that matches the learner's goal and level.
- Add one realistic constraint before the first learner turn.
- For challenge mode, add one complication after 2-3 successful learner turns.
- Keep the user's task practical: request, explain, negotiate, clarify, summarize, or close.

## Daily Life

- Housing: ask a landlord to fix a leaking sink; constraint: the landlord says the repair person is unavailable until next week.
- Banking: ask about a suspicious card charge; constraint: the bank needs identity verification first.
- Gym: cancel a membership; constraint: the contract says cancellation needs 30 days' notice.
- Hair salon: explain the haircut is shorter than requested; constraint: the stylist offers a discount instead of a fix.
- Delivery: report a missing package; constraint: tracking says it was delivered.

## Travel

- Airport: rebook after a missed connection; constraint: the next direct flight is full.
- Hotel: change rooms because of noise; constraint: only upgraded rooms are available.
- Immigration: explain travel purpose and hotel details; constraint: the officer asks for more specific plans.
- Car rental: dispute an extra cleaning fee; constraint: the company says photos show damage.
- Train station: ask for help after buying the wrong ticket; constraint: the ticket is non-refundable.

## Customer Service

- Online shopping: request a refund for a damaged item; constraint: the refund window has passed.
- Subscription: ask for a billing correction; constraint: support says the charge is automatic.
- Electronics: request repair for a faulty device; constraint: warranty status is unclear.
- Restaurant delivery: report the wrong order; constraint: the restaurant is closing soon.
- App support: explain a login issue; constraint: the agent misunderstands the problem at first.

## Workplace

- Project delay: explain the cause and propose a new timeline; constraint: the manager needs a client update today.
- Meeting: ask for clarification without sounding unprepared; constraint: the topic is technical.
- Cross-team request: ask another team for help; constraint: they say they are already overloaded.
- Feedback: disagree with a proposed plan politely; constraint: the other person is senior.
- Status update: summarize progress, blockers, and next steps; constraint: keep it under one minute.

## Interviews

- Behavioral: answer "Tell me about a time you handled conflict"; constraint: the interviewer asks for measurable impact.
- Experience: explain a project clearly; constraint: the interviewer is non-technical.
- Weakness: discuss a weakness professionally; constraint: avoid sounding defensive.
- Salary: ask about compensation range; constraint: the recruiter wants your number first.
- Closing: ask thoughtful questions; constraint: the interview is nearly out of time.

## Business And Negotiation

- Vendor call: negotiate a discount; constraint: the vendor says pricing is fixed.
- Client update: explain a timeline risk; constraint: the client expects delivery this week.
- Sales objection: respond to "Your product is too expensive"; constraint: the buyer compares a cheaper competitor.
- Partnership: propose next steps after a first meeting; constraint: roles are not clear yet.
- Procurement: clarify contract terms; constraint: legal review may delay the deal.

## Healthcare

- Clinic appointment: describe symptoms and ask about next steps; constraint: appointment slots are limited.
- Pharmacy: ask about dosage and side effects; constraint: the pharmacist needs the prescription details.
- Insurance: ask whether a treatment is covered; constraint: the policy language is confusing.
- Follow-up: explain symptoms did not improve; constraint: the doctor asks for a timeline.

## Writing Tasks

- Complaint email: damaged product, delayed service, billing mistake, noisy hotel room.
- Workplace message: delay update, meeting summary, request for help, deadline negotiation.
- Interview email: thank-you note, availability reply, follow-up after no response.
- Customer support reply: apology, clarification, escalation, refund explanation.
- Professional profile: LinkedIn summary, project description, short bio.
